Sample:
ArchiveDocuments

Description:
This sample shows how to archive all documents from a folder and it's subfolders recursively.

Therefore™ Web service operations:
- CreateDocument
- StartCreateDocumentBatch
- UploadDocStreamToBatch
- SubmitCreateDocumentBatch

Requirements:
* Assure that the "Files" category has been imported into your Therefore system.
* Make sure the category and field numbers in the file Common.CategoryIds.cs are valid.
